About TechshipProto

Empowering small organizations through better financial management

Our Mission

TechshipProto was created with a simple but powerful mission: to provide sports teams and small organizations with professional-grade financial management tools that are easy to use and affordable.

We believe that lack of resources shouldn't stand in the way of great organizations doing great work. Whether you're running a youth sports league, an amateur club, or a community organization, you deserve access to the same quality tools that larger enterprises use.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, TechshipProto emerged from a common frustration: managing the finances of a small sports team was unnecessarily complicated. Spreadsheets were scattered, receipts were lost, and tracking who paid their membership fees was a constant headache.

We set out to build a better solution. One that would centralize everything in one place, make financial tracking simple, and give small organizations the insights they need to make smart decisions about their money.

Today, hundreds of teams trust TechshipProto to manage their finances, track their members, and keep their organizations running smoothly.

Our Values

Simplicity

Financial management should be straightforward, not complicated. We design for clarity and ease of use.

Accessibility

Every organization deserves great tools, regardless of their size or budget.

Reliability

Your data is precious. We ensure it's always secure, backed up, and accessible when you need it.

Community
